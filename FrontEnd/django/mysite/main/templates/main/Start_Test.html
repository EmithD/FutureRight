Openness

(refers to a person's willingness to consider new ideas, experiences, and perspectives. It involves being open-minded and receptive to different ways of thinking.)




Neuroticism

(Neuroticism refers to a personality trait characterized
by emotional instability, anxiety, moodiness, and
a tendency towards negative thinking.)




Conscientiousness

(Conscientiousness refers to a personality trait
characterized by being responsible, organized, and
dependable.It involves a strong sense of duty and a desire
to achieve goals through hard work and self-discipline.)




Agreeableness

(Agreeableness refers to a personality trait
characterized by being friendly, cooperative, and
compassionate towards others. It involves valuing
social harmony and prioritizing the needs of others.)




Extraversion

(Extraversion refers to a personality trait
characterized by outgoing, talkative, and assertive
behavior. People who score high in extraversion
tend to be sociable and enjoy being
around other people.)
